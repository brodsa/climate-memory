Climate memory

The Climate Memory game was developed to raise the awareness of the climate change. The game attempts to comunicate the facts of the human activities effecting and even destroying our nature, in a readable and understandable form. In addition, tips and suggestions are provided on what we can do ourselves to minimilize the negative impact on nature. We cannot wait for the others and goverments to make the changes - we,you are the change!

How to play

The play board consist of 12 cards in total, i.e. six card pairs. A pair is represented by a card containing an image and a card containing a text description of that image. The goal is to find all six matchies without any time limation. Please read the text carefully so that you can match the card easily.
