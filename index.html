Climate memory

The Climate Memory game was developed to raise the awareness of the climate change. The game attempts to comunicate the facts of the human activities effecting and even destroying our nature, in an entertaining form.

Would you like to play with the cards of yours. Sent me your suggestions about climate change facts or tips. An intersting content, which could help to rise the awareness about this topic, is more than welcome!

How to play

The play board consist of 12 cards in total, i.e. six card pairs. A pair is represented by a card containing an image and a card containing a text description of that image. The goal is to find all six matchies without any time limation. Please read the text carefully so that you can match the card easily.
